Origami

Origami is a fascinating art form that has been around for centuries. I find myself particularly drawn to modular origami, which involves the creation of complex structures and shapes using multiple pieces of paper. The precision and patience required to fold each piece and then assembling them together to form a larger structure is both challenging and rewarding. It allows for a great deal of experimentation and customization to create unique designs.

  • Modular Origami is my favorite
  • My goal is to one day complete a Senbazuru set
